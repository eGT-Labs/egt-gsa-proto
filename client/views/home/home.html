Helping researchers search and explore OpenFDA data on drug labels & adverse events.

Search Drug Labels

Catalyst offers researchers the ability search the labeling datasets, granting access to all of the OpenFDA records.

Filter by Facets

Catalyst offers researchers the ability to filter results using our unique search facets, enabling drill down results.

View details of Drug Labels

Catalysts offers researchers the ability to view details on the drug label in an interactive way.

Compare Adverse Event PRR and Frequency

Catalyst offers researchers the ability to compare the proportional reporting ratio of adverse events to the frequency of occurrence. Explore this graph below, which illustrates higher rates of adverse events associated with Lisinopril than in other substances, the majority of which occur in less than 5% of the sample.


Examine Adverse Event Frequency by Gender

Catalyst offers researchers the ability to compare the combined adverse event frequency of a particular drug by gender. Explore this graph below on Lisinopril, which illustrates a fairly even distribution of adverse events between males and females.


Examine Adverse Events by Weight Class Average

Catalyst offers researchers the ability to view the frequency of adverse events of a particular substance against the average frequency of adverse events by weight class. Explore this graph below on Lisinopril, which illustrates a higher rate of incidences among higher weight classes.
